HELP MAKE THE MUSIC HAPPEN!

Our volunteers make the Mile of Music happen! Be sure to visit them and say thanks

To help make an event as big and dynamic as the Mile of Music happen, we need between 200-300 volunteers in all kinds of roles. Please read below to see how you can help Make the Music Happen.

HOW DOES VOLUNTEERING WORK?

We are looking for friendly, hardworking people who are excited to show what our Downtown and community can offer to the artists/bands and many visitors who will be coming from across the state, Midwest, and nation to experience the Mile of Music festival.

Most volunteer shifts will be 5-6 hours in length. Volunteers will be given special name badge lanyards to wear that denote their important role with the festival. The volunteer name badges will also provide volunteers with:

  • Special price for a Mile of Music t-shirt;
  • Updates and inside information on the latest festival news as it happens;
  • An invitation to a special post-festival Volunteer & Sponsors celebration;
  • Other perks yet to be announced.

Here is a listing of some of the volunteer roles we need your help with (you’ll find more detailed descriptions for each position at the bottom of the page): Pre-Festival Preparation | Venue Captain | Door/Ticketing | Band/Stage Volunteers | Houdini Plaza Volunteers | Festival Roamer/ Gopher | Info Booth/Merchandise | Band/VIP Lounge | Transportation

HOW DO I BECOME A VOLUNTEER?

After reviewing the volunteer role descriptions, send us an email letting us know:

  • your availability during the festival (August 7-10);
  • how many shifts you are willing/able to work; and
  • the top 2-3 volunteer roles you would be able to assist with.

While we won’t be able to accommodate everybody’s schedule entirely, we’ll certainly try our best. We definitely appreciate multiple shifts because the more experienced the volunteer, the better, at this major event.

DETAILED VOLUNTEER ROLE DESCRIPTIONS:

Pre-Festival Preparation

  • Helps prepare band information and registration materials
  • Prepares VIP grab bags
  • Assists with other pre-festival preparation needs

Venue Captain
(must be 21+ or with parent/legal guardian)

  • Primary festival representative onsite during shows at venue
  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for band and concert attendees
  • Works with venue management and staff to address issues that may arise -ID checking, Capacity, and Security are the responsibility of the venue and not the volunteer -Venue Captain will alert venue management/staff if something needs to be taken care related to hospitality, security or an emergency
  • Respond to needs and requests of band/artist
  • Communicates with production team (A/V) to ensure highest sound and performance quality
  • Makes sure Venue Sponsor signage (when applicable) is displayed and visible in venue during performances Reminds band/artists and attendees that Mile of Music is a “cover free” zone featuring all original music

Door/Ticketing
(must be 21+ or with parent/legal guardian at select venues)

  • Takes/scans tickets at Headline and Feature Stage performances
  • Ensures accurate count of capacity/attendance at Headline and Feature Stage venues
  • Provides excellent customer service and assistance to concert attendees

Band/Stage Volunteers
(must be 21+ for some venues)

  • Assists bands/artists loading in and packing up equipment before and after performances
  • Provides assistance to Production & A/V Teams
  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for bands/artists before, during, and after performances

Houdini Plaza Volunteers
(must be 21+)

  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for band and concert attendees
  • Assists in wrist banding and selling beverages during performances in Houdini Plaza
  • Follows direction/instruction from Appleton Downtown, Inc. volunteer supervisor
  • Responsible for proper license identification for wristband/beer sales
  • Sells and serves beverages
  • Provides excellent customer service and assistance

Info Booth/Merchandise Sales

  • Provides excellent customer service and assistance to festival attendees
  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for band and festival attendees
  • Serves as a resource for bands and festival attendees
  • Sells Mile of Music Merchandise

Festival Roamer/Gopher

  • Provides excellent customer service and assistance to festival attendees
  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for band and festival attendees
  • Walks designated area of the Mile and serves as a source of information for attendees; also serves as a look out for possible issues, emergencies, etc…and reports them to the APD
  • Monitors lines outside of venues and reports status back to headquarters
  • Frequently checks in performances to ensure things are going well and to see if Venue Captains need assistance
  • Keeps Mile of Music Headquarters updated about status of their portion of the Mile
  • Gets whatever needs to be taken care of done

Band/VIP Lounge

  • Provides excellent customer service and assistance to bands/artists and VIPs at Lounge
  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for bands and VIPs
  • Assists in registering bands/artists for festival
  • Helps VIPs pick up their lanyard badges, grab bags and other festival related info
  • Serves as a resource for bands and VIPs
  • Sells Mile of Music merchandise
  • Assists onsite catering to ensure food/drink is available, well-stocked, and fresh

Transportation
(must be 21+ with valid driver’s license)

  • Provides excellent customer service and assistance to festival attendees
  • Ensures exceptional hospitality for band and festival attendees
  • Provides transportation to bands/artists and Mile of Music Media Team between lodging and performance venues
