HELP MAKE THE MUSIC HAPPEN!

Our volunteers make the Mile of Music happen! When you see them be sure to say hello and thanks!

To make an event as big and dynamic as the Mile of Music happen, we need LOTS of volunteers-in fact, about 200 or so-in all kinds of roles. Please read below to see how you can help Make the Music Happen.

HOW DOES VOLUNTEERING WORK?

We are looking for friendly, hardworking people who are excited to show what our Downtown and community can offer to artists/bands and the many visitors who will be coming from across the state, Midwest, and nation to experience the Mile of Music festival.

Most volunteer shifts will be 4-6 hours in length. Volunteers will be given special name badge lanyards to wear that denote their important role with the festival. The volunteer name badges will also provide volunteers with:

  • Free admission for each volunteer at the special bonus kick-off concert on Wednesday night, August 6th at the Fox Cities PAC;
  • Special price for one of our Mile of Music souvenir t-shirts;
  • Updates and inside information on the latest festival news as it happens;
  • An invitation to a special post-festival Volunteer & Sponsors celebration;
  • Other perks yet to be announced.

Here is a listing of some of the volunteer roles we need your help with (you'll find more detailed descriptions for each position here): Pre-Festival Preparation | Venue Captain | Door/Ticketing | Band/Stage Volunteers | Houdini Plaza Volunteers | Festival Roamer/ Gopher | Info Booth/Merchandise | Band/VIP Lounge | Transportation
