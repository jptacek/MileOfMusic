The VIP Priority Access Pass includes:

  • Priority Access to 14 Lead Band Venues (Lawrence Memorial Chapel; Anduzzi's Patio; Appleton Beer Factory; Chadwick's, CU Saloon, Deja Vu; Durty Leprechaun; Emmett's; Fox River House; Luna Lounge; Mill Creek; Skyline Comedy/Music Club; The Bar; X-TRA 920) as capacity allows;

  • TWO tickets per pass for the Aug. 6 Women Shine bonus kickoff concert at 7:30 p.m. at the Fox Cities PAC (bring a friend!);
  • Exclusive Admission to First-Ever Mile of Music Jam: A Songwriter Salute to Tom Petty at the Radisson Grand Ballroom at 9:30 p.m. on Aug. 7;
  • Exclusive Access to performing artists at the Trilliant Food, Beverage, & Nutrition Artist & VIP Lounge (located in the Fox Cities Performing Arts Center's Kimberly-Clark Theater, 400 W. College Ave.);
  • Priority Access to Song Before We Go closing event at 4 p.m. on Aug. 10 at the Lawrence Memorial Chapel;
  • Special notifications of pop-up events and special offerings downtown during the festival;
  • And more!

VIP Vouchers must be exchanged for VIP Passes during the week of the Festival at our Mile of Music Headquarters from Monday, August 4 through Sunday, August 10, located at the Fox Cities Performing Arts Center (signage will direct you once you are there). Hours will be: Monday/Tuesday, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.; Wednesday, Aug. 6, 10 a.m. to 7:30 p.m.; Thursday through Saturday, 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.; Sunday, 10 a.m. to 4 p.m

Important Note:

For each individual VIP Pass purchased, you receive TWO tickets to the Wednesday, Aug. 6 Women Shine bonus kickoff concert at the Fox Cities Performing Arts Center, which starts at 7:30 p.m. We ask that you give the tickets to a friend or colleague should you not be able to use them. The concert will feature five emerging female-led acts and promises to be a special night, hosted by Cory Chisel and Hillary Reynolds.

And, did you know your VIP Priority Access Pass is transferable? In other words, if you can't use it on a specific day, you can offer it to someone else to use for a specific show or a specific day, etc. But be sure to get it back, because only one pass will be issued per voucher with no exceptions.

All venues have limited capacities. With a VIP Priority Access Pass, please note that your access to shows is based on available capacity, but does include priority access for the larger, indoor band venues. For most smaller venues, seating is on a first-come, first-served basis and open to all.

**PLEASE NOTE THAT THE $150.00 VIP Priority Access Pass PRICE INCLUDES ALL CONVENIENCE AND HANDLING FEES. Tickets are non-refundable. VIP Priority Access Passes do not guarantee admission to a particular venue.

There are 60 venues hosting music at Mile 2, with 14 of them serving as VIP Priority Access Venues. Though all festival attendees can attend VIP Priority Access venue performances if capacity allows at show time, VIP Priority Access pass-holders have FIRST access to these venues and also move to the front of any lines that may exist at these venues (similar to past passes at amusement parks). For performances at the Lawrence Memorial Chapel, VIP Priority Access lasts until 15 minutes prior to show time.

ARTISTS & SCHEDULE SUBJECT TO CHANGE:

Mile of Music is a handcrafted artisan festival showcasing original artists and their original music. It's a "cover-free zone!"" With more than 600 performances being scheduled, please understand that there may be changes to the lineups as things evolve for bands and performers leading up to the festival, and that all artist lineups are subject to change. We will keep you informed of any changes as they occur, and we reserve the right to substitute artists to complete lineups or to perform where other artists have had to cancel or rearrange their performance dates and times. As a music festival fan, we sincerely appreciate your understanding of this possibility as our festival continues to evolve.
